About Us

The NBA Dutse Branch Difference

The Dutse branch of the Nigerian Bar Association (NBA) was established a few years after Jigawa State was created from Kano State in 1991. Initially, the branch comprised lawyers working in the Jigawa State Ministry of Justice.

The pioneer interim chairman was Hon. Justice M.L. Shuaibu, who served in that capacity before Abubakar Adamu, another staff member of the Ministry of Justice, was elected as chairman in 2001. In 2003, Salisu Abdu, also a staff member of the Ministry of Justice, was elected as the chairman of the branch. However, before concluding his tenure, he received an appointment in Abuja and his deputy, Mr. Peter Omenka, succeeded him in an acting capacity until Sani Hussaini Garun Gabbas SAN was elected as the chairman. Notably, Garun Gabbas SAN was the first member to be elected as chairman from outside the Ministry of Justice. After serving his tenure, Musa M. Imam was elected as the chairman of the branch. Briefly, A.G. Saleh succeeded Mr. Imam, but soon left the branch to pursue a new career in Abuja, paving the way for Garun Gabbas SAN's second coming as chairman.He served from 2008-2010 and again had his mandate renewed as a result of which he held sway from 2010-2012.

Hon.Justice Adamu Turaki Muhammad was then elected as Chairman in the year 2012.He served from 2012-2014 and had another term from 2014-2015 just one year into the second tenure he was elevated to the Federal High Court bench in December 2015. Nuhu Suleiman Tafida then oversees the affairs of the Association as acting Chairman before handing over to Sani Hussaini Garun-Gabbas, SAN who as the then Attorney General and Commissioner for Justice, Jigawa State organized the transition that saw the emergence of Mr.Baffa Alasan as Chairman in 2016.Baffa Alasan served from 2016-2018. Upon completing his tenure in 2018,Mr.Baffa Alasan organized the transition which saw the emergence of Bashir Usaini as the Chairman of the branch in 2018.He served from 2018-2020. It was at that time after a botched transition that the then President of the Nigerian Bar Association Mr.Paul Usoro constitited a caretaker Committee for the branch under the Chairman of of Nuhu Suleiman Tafida who relinquished the Chairmanship of the Caretaker Committee and handed over to Baffa Alasan for him to contest election in the transition organized by the Caretaker Committee.

In the late 2020,the Baffa Alasan led Caretaker Committee conducted election which saw the emergence of Nuhu Suleiman Tafida as Chairman of the branch. He served in that capacity until 28th September, 2023 when he handed over to Mr.Mustapha Mohammed Kashim having been duly elected as the Chairman of the branch.

Establishment

The NBA Dutse Branch was established in 1975, with a mission to uphold the principles of justice, legal integrity, and professional excellence.

The NBA Nigerian Bar Association Dutse Branch is a local chapter of the Nigerian Bar Association (NBA) located in Dutse, Nigeria. It serves as a platform for lawyers practicing in Dutse and its environs to network, collaborate, and advocate for legal issues pertinent to the region. The branch likely organizes various events, seminars, and workshops aimed at professional development and promoting the rule of law within the community.
